Ada's List

Ada’s List is to a list of tech firms and the percentages of female software engineers employed full-time residing in the continental united states. The largest firms who have the most impact are most important to helping the effort.

Tracy Chou pushed the first commit to the git repository women-in-eng on Oct 20, 2013. Since there have been countless organizations, initiatives, and efforts in bringing light to this very serious of employment equality. Resources, organizations, and conferences dedicated to addressing the various contributing factors to the issue are being conceived of by the day. This is another to fight the good fight.
