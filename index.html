Ada's List

Ada’s List is an open-source effort to keep a working list of tech firms and the percentages of female software engineers employed full-time residing in the continental United States. The largest firms who have the most impact are most important to helping the effort.

Tracy Chou pushed the first commit to the git repository women-in-eng on Oct 20, 2013 after posting "Where are the numbers?" her Medium article that got her her story heard. Since there have been countless organizations, initiatives, and efforts in bringing light to this very serious issue of employment equality.

Resources, meet-up groups, and mentorship programs dedicated to addressing the various contributing factors to the gender disparity are being conceived of by the day.

Here's another to fight the good fight.
