Ada's List

Ada’s List is an initiative to create open-source projects that support aspiring and existing female software engineers. Create working list of the perecntage of female software engineers employed full-time residing in the continental United States, using Tracy Chou's list, women-in-eng.

Tracy Chou pushed the first commit to the git repository women-in-eng on Oct 20, 2013 after posting "Where are the numbers?" her Medium article that got her her story heard. Since there has been a heightened consciousness of the issue within the tech community and the mainstream media. Interviews, confessionals and research on the matter has brought light to this serious issue of employment equality.

Resources, organizations, and mentorship programs dedicated to addressing the various contributing factors to the gender disparity are being conceived of by the day.

Here's another to fight the good fight.
